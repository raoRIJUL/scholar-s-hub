Army Institute Of Technology

Army Institute Of Technology Pune offers several scholarships on the basis of category,

merit and financial condition of the students

Scholarships for first year students

ESSA

Eligibility Criteria :

Wards of serving Army personnel and MNS Offrs who have not attained 25 years of age at the time of completion of class/course from Class IX to PG level. (Wards of Retired Army personnel, TA personnel, battle casualty and deceased Army personnel are not eligible).

Scholarship Amount : Rs 6,000/-

Gender : Boys & Girls.

For more information click here

PRAGATI

Eligibility Criteria :

(a)Admitted to 1st year degree program
(b) Family income less than Rs.8 Lakh
(c) Max two girls child per family are eligible
(d) The gap period between the year of passing the qualifying exam and the year of admission shall not be more than two years

Scholarship Amount : Tuition fee - Rs 30,000/- and Rs 2,000/- pm for 10 months as incidental charges

Gender : girls only

For more information click here

PMSS

Eligibility Criteria :

(a) Dependent wards / widows of Ex Servicemen
(b) First Year students only
(c) Students must apply online on KSB web portal www.ksb.gov.in

Scholarship Amount : Boys - 2,500/- pm and Girls - 3,000/- pm

Gender : Boys and Girls.

For more information click here

SAKSHAM

Eligibility Criteria :

(a) Specially abled student, having disability not less than 40%
(b) Admitted to 1st year degree program
(c) Family income less than 8 Lakh

Scholarship Amount : Tuition fee - Rs 30,000/- and 2,000/- pm for 10 months as incidental charges

Gender : Specially abled Boys & Girls

For more information click here

DIAV Scholarship

Eligibility Criteria :

Wards of soldiers who died in harness

Scholarship Amount : 50,000/- and one time computer grant

Gender : Boys and Girls

UDCHALO

Eligibility Criteria :

(a)udChalo Scholarship Assessment 50% to Academics: Marks secured in theory papers only
(b)35% Enterprising Aptitude: No. of tech and cultural events participated (won) outside the college
(c)15% Additional Responsibilities: Important positions / activities anchored during the applicable period
(d)Only regular students enrolled in the mentioned discipline of engineering at Army Institute of Technology, Pune
(e)Students participating in this scheme must not have a year down

Scholarship Amount : 1st Shortlist - Rs 15,000/- and Shortlist - Rs 10,000/-

Gender : boys and girls

PERSISTENT SYSTEMS

Eligibility Criteria :

FE Comp/IT girl, Min 85% in PCM (HSC), Family income - max Rs 3 LPA, continuation of scholarship subject to scoring min 60% marks without any backlog in the previous year

Scholarship Amount :40,000
