Scholarships for SE,TE,BE students

HASHMAP TECH INDIA PVT LTD

Eligibility Criteria :

(a) Scholarship will be granted to students of second year onwards (b) Should have no backlogs. (c) Should have no score min 7.0 GPA in previous year University examination. (d) Minimum attendance to be 75 % in the preceding academic year. (e)No involvement in any disciplinary case in AIT or outside. (f) Economic criteria will have overbearing weightage. Precedence for award of this scholarship will be:- i) Wards of martyrs ii) Wards of widows/divorcees iii) Wards of retired ORs iv) Wards of retired JCOs v) Wards of serving ORs vi) Wards of serving JCOs

Scholarship Amount : 15,000

Gender : Boys and Girls
